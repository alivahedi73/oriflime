Frequently Asked Questions

What’s a VIP Customer?

When you shop at Oriflame and head to checkout, you can choose to remain a guest or become a VIP customer. And when you enroll as a VIP, you get access to a wide range of benefits and free perks.

How do I become a VIP Customer?

Simply register for free when you make a purchase on our website. At checkout, you’ll be prompted to create an account.

If you’re interested in selling our products, you can sign up to become an Oriflame Brand Partner. Click on “Sign Up” in the main navigation to learn more.

What benefits do I get as a VIP?

There are a whole host of benefits and free perks that put you in control of your beauty like never before, and reward you for doing what you already do.

• Get matched with an Oriflame Brand Partner, who will give you personalised skin care and beauty advice.
• Collect Beauty Reward Points for every purchase and redeem them for free products.
• Get free shipping on all orders over £45.
• Get early access to new product launches and offers.
• Create a healthy routine with AutoShip and get 5% off skin care sets or every fourth wellness product free.
• 30-day money-back guarantee.

Do VIP Customers get any discounts?

Yes! You’ll be the first to know about special offers and discounts. And you get 5% off our premium NovAge Skin Care sets when you sign up for our Auto-Ship programme.

Can I return products?

Yes, you can. In fact, we think you’ll love your new beauty products so much, we have a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you’re not satisfied for some reason, you can return them. Head to the Order History section of your account after login, and fill in an online form.

How do I pay for my orders?

You can pay with a debit/credit card or use a PayPal account.

Can I still buy products if I’m not a VIP Customer?

Of course you can! You can shop as a guest customer or through your Brand Partner. You simply won’t have access to the perks and rewards that VIPs get.

What delivery options do I have?

We offer standard UK postal delivery to your home or the delivery address of your choice.

How does AutoShip work?

When you buy one of our NovAge Skin Care sets or our wellness supplements/food, you can choose to enroll in our AutoShip programme at checkout. That’s when you pick the frequency with which you want to receive your products – every three, six or nine weeks. Read more about AutoShip here.
